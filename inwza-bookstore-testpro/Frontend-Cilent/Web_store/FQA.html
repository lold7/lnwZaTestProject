FAQs

Common questions about orders, shipping, returns, and your account.

We ship most orders within two business days. Standard delivery takes five to seven business days depending on your location. Express options are available at checkout for faster delivery.

Books may be returned within thirty days of purchase if they remain unread and in original condition. Return shipping is free. Once received, refunds are processed within five business days.

Orders can be modified within one hour of placement. Contact us immediately with your order number and requested changes. After one hour, the order enters our fulfillment process and cannot be altered.

Yes, gift wrapping is available for most items at checkout. Select your preferred wrapping style and include a personal message if desired. This service is complimentary on orders over fifty dollars.

Click the login link in the navbar and select "Forgot password." Enter your email address and we'll send a reset link within minutes. Follow the instructions to create a new password.
