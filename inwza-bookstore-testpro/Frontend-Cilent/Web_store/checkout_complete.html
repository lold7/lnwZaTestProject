Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about payment and delivery

How long does shipping take

Standard delivery arrives within five to seven business days. Express shipping takes two business days. International orders may require additional time depending on customs clearance.

What payment methods do you accept

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and digital wallets. Your payment information is encrypted and processed securely through our trusted payment partner.

Can I change my order after placing it

Orders can be modified within one hour of placement. After that, contact our support team immediately to request changes before your order ships.

Do you ship internationally

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. Customs duties may apply depending on your country.

What is your return policy

Books in new condition can be returned within thirty days for a full refund. Opened or damaged books may be eligible for exchange. Return shipping is free on qualifying items.
