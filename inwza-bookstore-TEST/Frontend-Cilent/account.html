Help

Find answers to common questions about managing your account.

How do I reset my password?

Click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address and follow the instructions sent to your inbox. You'll receive a secure link to create a new password within minutes.

Can I change my email address?

Yes. Go to your account settings and select "Email Address." Enter your new email and verify it through the confirmation link we send you. Your account will be updated immediately.

How is my payment information protected?

We use industry-standard encryption to protect all payment data. Your card details are never stored on our servers. We comply with PCI DSS security standards to keep your information safe.

What happens to my order history?

Your complete order history is saved in your account indefinitely. You can view past purchases, track shipments, and reorder items anytime. This information is private and accessible only to you.

Can I delete my account?

You can request account deletion from your settings. We'll remove your personal information within thirty days, though order history may be retained for legal and tax purposes.

Still have questions?

Reach out to our support team anytime.
